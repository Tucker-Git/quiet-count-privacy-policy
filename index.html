Privacy Policy for Quiet Count

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Summary: Quiet Count does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device only.

Information Collection and Use

Quiet Count is a countdown timer extension that operates entirely on your local device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information or user data.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device only using Chrome's storage API:

  • Theme Preference: Your selected visual theme (Light, Dark, Solarized, or Cat)
  • Favorite Timers: Timer durations you've saved for quick access

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Can be cleared by removing the extension or clearing Chrome's extension data

Permissions

Storage: Used only to save your theme preference and favorite timer durations locally on your device.

Notifications: Used only to alert you when a countdown timer completes. No data is collected through notifications.

Third-Party Services

Quiet Count does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.
